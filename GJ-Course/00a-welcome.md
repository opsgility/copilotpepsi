# Welcome & Introductions (15 min)
---

> **PPTX Reference:** This section corresponds to **Slides 2–7** of the GJ_Pepsi_Copilot_Workshop.pptx deck.

### About This Workshop

This full-day hands-on workshop is built specifically for G&J Pepsi. Every exercise uses scenarios from your business — Q4 sales data, warehouse operations, onboarding guides, supply chain reviews, and IT infrastructure plans.

**What we'll cover:**
- Copilot Chat, Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams, PowerPoint, and Copilot Studio Agents
- A prompting framework you'll practice in every exercise
- Department-specific scenarios for Accounting, HR, IT, Sales, Operations, and Supply Chain

### Ground Rules

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Full-day workshop** | 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM. Hands-on exercises throughout. You'll use Copilot in your own Microsoft 365 environment. |
| **Be present** | Close email and other apps during exercises — the practice time is where the learning happens. |
| **Ask questions anytime** | This is a working session. Interrupt with questions as they come up. |
| **Breaks** | Morning break, lunch, and afternoon break built into the schedule. |
| **Cross-functional audience** | Exercises include scenarios for every department. Try scenarios outside your comfort zone too. |
