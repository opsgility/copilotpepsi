# Exercise 6: PowerPoint — Create Presentations with AI (45 min)
---

Copilot in PowerPoint helps you create professional presentations quickly. You can:

- **Create from a prompt** — Describe what you need and Copilot builds a full deck with slides, layouts, and content.
- **Create from a Word document** — Transform a written document into a presentation automatically.
- **Add and edit slides** — Ask Copilot to add new slides, reorganize content, or generate speaker notes.
- **Design suggestions** — Copilot uses the Designer feature to apply professional layouts.

---

### Task 1: Create a presentation from a prompt (15 min)

You need to create a presentation for an all-hands meeting about Q4 performance and next quarter's priorities.

1. In **PowerPoint for the web**, create a new blank presentation.

    ```
    https://powerpoint.cloud.microsoft/
    ```
2. Select **Create a presentation about...** (or look for the Copilot prompt in the Home tab) and enter:

    ```
    Create a presentation for a G&J Pepsi quarterly all-hands meeting. Include slides for: (1) Q4 highlights and key wins, (2) Revenue performance vs. targets by region, (3) Operational excellence updates — warehouse efficiency and safety, (4) Employee spotlight and recognition, (5) Key initiatives for next quarter, (6) Q&A. Use a professional, energetic tone. Include placeholder text where specific data would go.
    ```

3. If prompted, click **Generate slides** then **Keep it**. 

4. Review the generated presentation. Copilot should create 10-30 slides with content, layouts, and design elements.

---

### Task 2: Create a presentation from a Word document (15 min)

Transform the route optimization proposal into a presentation for leadership.

1. Create a new blank presentation in **PowerPoint for the web**.
2. Select the **Copilot** icon and choose **Create a new presentation with file...**
3. Attach the **GJ_Route_Optimization_Proposal.docx** file from your OneDrive.
4. Enter the following and click **Generate new presentation**. 

    ```
    Create a presentation from this route optimization proposal. Target audience: senior leadership. Focus on the business case, expected ROI, implementation timeline, and risk mitigation. Keep it concise — no more than 10 slides.
    ```

5. Click **Open presentation** if prmpted. Review the generated deck. Copilot should transform the document's sections into visual slides.

### Task 3: Create a department-specific presentation (15 min)

Create a presentation relevant to your role. Choose one:

**HR / Learning & Development:**
> "Create a 6-slide presentation for new employee orientation at G&J Pepsi. Include: company history and mission, organizational structure, key policies, benefits overview, first-week expectations, and a 'welcome to the team' closing slide. Make it welcoming and visually engaging."

**Accounting / Finance:**
> "Create an 8-slide presentation for a quarterly finance review. Include: revenue summary, expense breakdown, budget vs. actual comparison, cash flow highlights, key financial risks, and recommendations. Include placeholder charts."

**IT:**
> Attach the **GJ_IT_Infrastructure_Upgrade_Plan.docx** file and prompt: "Create a presentation from this IT upgrade plan. Target audience: business stakeholders, not IT staff. Focus on business impact, timeline, what employees will notice, and the support plan."

**Sales / Retail:**
> "Create a 7-slide presentation for a new product launch briefing. The product is a new zero-sugar energy drink entering convenience store channels. Include: market opportunity, product details, pricing strategy, promotional plan, sales targets by region, and retailer talking points."

**Operations / Supply Chain:**
> "Create a presentation for a warehouse safety stand-down meeting. Include: recent incident trends, top 3 safety focus areas, new protocols being implemented, recognition of safety champions, and a call to action. Tone: serious but motivating."

For whichever scenario you choose:
1. Generate the initial presentation
2. Refine at least 2 slides using follow-up prompts
3. Add speaker notes to the opening slide

---

### Tips for Better PowerPoint Results

- **Be specific about slide count** — "Create a 10-slide presentation" gives better results than "Create a presentation."
- **Specify your audience** — Copilot adjusts detail level and language based on who will see it.
- **Reference files for accuracy** — When creating from a document, Copilot pulls real content instead of generating placeholders.
- **Iterate slide by slide** — After the initial creation, refine individual slides rather than asking Copilot to redo the whole deck.
- **Use Designer** — After Copilot creates content, use PowerPoint's Designer feature (in the ribbon) to apply polished visual layouts.
